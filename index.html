About CHCC



CHCC

The Center for Human-Centered Computing (CHCC) is an interdisciplinary research collective that conducts cutting-edge research to design technology with and for people. We take a participatory, user-centered approach to building systems that respond to real-world needs.

At CHCC, our work spans across domains in Human-AI interaction, mobile sensing, healthcare, education, ubiquitous computing, and digital public infrastructure. We develop technologies that are context-aware, culturally grounded, and aimed at driving meaningful societal impact.

We also collaborate across academia, government, and not-for-profits to ensure that our solutions are both research-driven and rooted in everyday realities.




Product Strategy Unit
